About Madrasa

Your Gateway to Islamic
Learning and Growth

Madrasa App is a holistic platform for Muslims, offering Islamic education, counseling, prayer tools, and community engagement. From live classes and spiritual guidance to Umrah planning and news, it unites the Ummah through faith, knowledge, and technology, empowering a connected and inspired community.

Features

One app for education,
prayer, and daily needs of the Ummah.

Online learning

Madrasa App helps you learn the Quran, Islamic studies, and languages with expert tutors. Study online or offline at your own pace and grow in knowledge and faith.

Online Guidance

Facing challenges in life? Struggling with relationships or parenting? Madrasa App connects you with Islamic scholars for guidance, clarity, and peace. Your companion for wellness and support.

Madrasa TV

Madrasa TV is your go-to platform for Islamic content. Enjoy live events, videos, sermons, documentaries, and more. Fun and accessible learning for you and your family.

Prayer Tools

Madrasa App offers tools like the Digital Quran, Namaz Alerts, Dua Collection, and Zakat Calculator. Stay connected to your faith effortlessly and strengthen your Imaan daily.
